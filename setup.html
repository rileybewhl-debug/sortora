Set up in under 5 minutes

Add split payments to your booking page with one script tag. No coding required — just copy, paste, and start collecting.

Watch the 60-second setup walkthrough
Four steps to split payments
1
Create your free account
Sign up at sortora.com and connect your Stripe account. This is how you'll receive payouts from split payments — money goes directly to your bank.
~ 2 minutes
2
Copy your widget code
Go to the Widget page in your dashboard. You'll see a script tag with your unique business ID. Copy it.
<script
  src="https://sortora.com/widget.js"
  data-business-id="your-business-id"
  data-theme="auto"
></script>
~ 30 seconds
3
Paste it on your booking page
Add the script tag to your website — right before the closing </body> tag on any page where you want split payments. Works with WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Shopify, or any custom site.
WordPress users: Go to Appearance → Theme Editor → footer.php. Paste the code right before </body>. For Squarespace, go to Settings → Advanced → Code Injection → Footer.
~ 1 minute
4
Start collecting split payments
That's it. Your customers will see a "Split this" button on your booking page. They enter group members, everyone pays their share, and you get the full amount deposited to your bank account via Stripe.
Pro tip: Create a test split from your dashboard to see the full flow — payment links, email notifications, and payout tracking — before going live with customers.
You're live!
