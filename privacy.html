Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

What we collect

When you create a Sortora business account, we collect your name, email address, and business name. When participants pay through a Sortora split, we collect their name and email address to coordinate the payment.

We do not collect or store credit card numbers, bank account details, or any payment card data. All payment processing is handled directly by Stripe, our PCI Level 1 certified payment processor.

How we use your data

We use your information to operate the Sortora platform: creating booking sessions, sending payment link emails, processing split payments, and confirming bookings. We also use your email to send transactional messages like payment confirmations and booking updates.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

Sortora uses the following third-party services to operate:

Stripe processes all payments. When you pay through Sortora, your payment data is handled by Stripe under their privacy policy. Sortora never receives your card details.

Supabase hosts our database and authentication system. Your account data is stored in Supabase's encrypted, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure.

Vercel hosts our website and API. All connections use TLS encryption.

Resend delivers transactional emails (payment links, confirmations). Your email address is shared with Resend solely for email delivery.

Data security

All data transmitted to and from Sortora is encrypted with TLS 1.2 or higher. We enforce HSTS headers to prevent connection downgrades. Our API endpoints are protected with rate limiting, CORS enforcement, and input validation. For full details, see our Security page.

Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Booking session data is retained for 12 months after the booking date for record-keeping purposes. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing privacy@sortora.com.

Cookies

Sortora uses essential cookies for authentication (maintaining your login session). We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also request a copy of all data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@sortora.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, contact us at privacy@sortora.com.
