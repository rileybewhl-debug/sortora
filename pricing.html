Frequently asked questions

How does pricing work? Who pays the fees?
On the free Starter plan, a small convenience fee ($0.50 + 1.5%) is added to each participant's share — your customers pay it, not you. On paid plans, you choose: absorb the fee for a seamless customer experience, or pass it through. Either way, you always receive the full booking amount via Stripe.
What happens if not everyone pays their share?
You're never at risk. Sortora tracks each participant's payment status in real time. The booking only confirms when all shares are collected. If someone doesn't pay within 7 days, the split expires automatically. No partial charges — it's all or nothing.
How long does setup take?
Under 5 minutes. Add one script tag to your booking page, connect your Stripe account, and you're live. No developer needed. If you do have a developer, there's a full API for custom integrations.
Is Sortora secure?
All payments are processed through Stripe, the same infrastructure used by Amazon, Google, and Shopify. Card data never touches Sortora's servers — it goes directly to Stripe via their PCI-compliant checkout. We're SAQ-A compliant with full encryption and security headers on every request.
Can I customize how the widget looks?
Yes. Growth and Scale plans include custom branding — match your brand colors, fonts, and style. The widget uses Shadow DOM isolation so it won't conflict with your site's CSS. Choose from inline embed, modal popup, or shareable link modes.
What's your refund policy?
Cancel anytime — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Paid plans are prorated to the day. For split payments, refunds are handled through your Stripe dashboard since funds go directly to your connected account.
What if I exceed my plan's split limit?
On Starter, you'll need to upgrade. On Growth and Scale, overages are charged at a slightly higher per-split rate. Or upgrade to the next tier for a lower blended rate — we'll notify you when it makes sense.
Do my customers need to create an account?
No. Participants just click the payment link, see the booking details and their share, and pay. Email only, no signup required. Apple Pay and Google Pay are supported for one-tap checkout.
