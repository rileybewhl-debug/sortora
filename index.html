How it works
Three steps. Five minutes.
That's all it takes.
1
Install the widget
Add one script tag to your booking page. Connect your Stripe account. No developer needed — if you can paste a line of code, you're done.
your-booking-page.html
<!-- Your existing checkout page -->
<div class="checkout-form">
  <h2>Complete your booking</h2>
  <button type="submit">Book Now</button>

  <!-- Add Sortora — just this one line -->
  <script src="https://js.sortora.com/v1/widget.js"
    data-business-id="your_id"></script>
</div>
That's the entire integration
2
Customer toggles split
At checkout, a split-payment toggle appears. Your customer enters their group's emails. Each person receives a unique payment link.
