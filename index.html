Why we built this

Everyone wanted the photos.
Nobody wanted to pay first.

Our team played the whole season. At the end, everyone said they'd chip in for a photographer. Then someone had to actually book it — and pay $280 upfront while hoping 14 other people would pay them back.

Nobody booked the photographer. We got no photos.

That's the problem Sortora fixes. The group funds it together — and nobody pays a cent until it's fully covered.
