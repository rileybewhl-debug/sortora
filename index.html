How it works
Three steps. Five minutes.
Install once, benefit on every group booking forever. No code changes to your existing checkout.
1

Install the widget

Add one script tag to your booking page. Connect your Stripe account. Takes under 5 minutes — no developer needed.

2

Customer toggles split

At checkout, your customer enters their group's emails. Each person receives a unique payment link for their share.

3

Everyone pays, booking confirms

When all shares are paid, the booking automatically confirms. You receive the full amount via Stripe. Done.
