How it works

Four steps to a session
that runs itself

✦
AI builds your schedule
Tell Sortora your service and availability. The AI generates recurring sessions — every Monday, every Saturday, whatever fits — instantly bookable.
📋
Members sign up
Share your session link. Participants mark themselves Going, Maybe, or Out. Their funding contribution is automatically calculated by status.
💳
Group funds it together
Each person pays their share directly. Funds are held in escrow. The session confirms automatically once the funding threshold is hit.
✓
Show up & get paid
Session happens, funds release to the provider. No chasing invoices, no awkward group chats, no one fronting cash for the whole team.
