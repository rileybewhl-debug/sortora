Service description

Sortora provides an embeddable split-payment widget that allows groups to divide booking costs. Businesses install the widget on their checkout pages. Customers use it to create split payments, share links with their group, and collect individual payments.

Sortora is a payment coordination platform. We do not process payments directly — all payment processing is handled by Stripe.

Transaction fees

Participant payments
